Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the AI assessment a diagnosis?
No, it is for reflection and guidance only. It is designed to help you organize your thoughts and suggest potential next steps.
Do I have to create an account to use this?
You can browse resources and professionals without an account, but saving your assessment progress or booking a consultation requires one.
How private is my information?
Extremely private. We meet strict health privacy standards and never sell your data or share it with professionals without your explicit consent.
Can I use Vivirion if I already have a therapist?
Yes! Our AI check-ins and evidence-based resources can be a great supplement between your therapy sessions.
